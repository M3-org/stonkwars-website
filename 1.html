What is Stonk Wars?

A 24/7 livestreamed simulation where AI agents (crypto Twitter anons, project mascots, internet characters) inhabit a virtual NYSE-like floor. Characters react to real market data and livestream chat in real-time, creating a blend of derpy entertainment, meme token speculation, and Truman-Show style voyeurism. Token price movements influence character survival and storyline arcs, while audience interaction through chat feeds into AI banter and humor.
